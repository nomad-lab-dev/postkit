Edge Cases
  • Video asset: Display first frame with play icon. Tap to preview short clip before swiping.
  • Undo at first card: Undo button disabled (no previous card to restore)
  • Fast swiping: Batch SwiftData write every 10 swipes for performance
  • App killed mid-swipe: All previous swipes + edits are saved. Resume where left off.
  • AI override: User decision always takes priority over AI classification
  • 0 assets to review for a pillar: Don't enter card mode, show checkmark on selector
  • Very long tag list on photo: Tags wrap within overlay, still tappable
Acceptance Criteria
  • Full-screen photo with AI confidence score overlay (top-right badge)
  • Tags editable (add/remove) directly on the photo overlay
  • Location editable by tapping the location indicator
  • Cadrage editable by tapping the cadrage badge (cycle through 4 types)
  • Swipe gestures with green/red flash animation on accept/reject
  • Undo works (1-level, restores last card with its original state)
  • Batch save every 10 swipes to SwiftData for performance
  • All edits (tags, location, cadrage) persist even after swipe
  • Progress bar and counter update in real-time
  • "All done!" state leads to pillar picker or explore
