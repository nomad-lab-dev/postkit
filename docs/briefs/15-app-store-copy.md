### Strategic recommendation

PostKit should **not** compete for "social media scheduler" or "instagram planner" — those are entrenched, high-difficulty keywords owned by funded companies with thousands of reviews. 

Instead, **own the "AI + content pillars + personal brand" intersection** — a triangle that no competitor currently occupies. The target user (solo founder, freelancer) actively searches "content pillars" and "personal brand" when researching content strategy. This is PostKit's uncontested territory.

**Primary battleground**: `content pillars` + `personal brand` + `AI social posts`  
**Secondary coverage**: `caption`, `hashtag`, `camera roll`, `daily posts`  
**Avoid**: `scheduler`, `planner`, `instagram`, `story`, `video` (contested, low ROI)
